Q&A

Q: How long does GPS positioning take?
A: The module usually takes more than 30 seconds for the first fix (cold start) in an open area.

Q: What does it mean if the screen shows a green "x4" after startup?
A: This represents an I2C communication failure, usually caused by poor soldering of the QMC5883 sensor or the header pins.

Q: Does it support charging with a dual-head Type-C cable?
A: You need to solder the corresponding CC1/CC2 pull-down resistors on the PCB to recognize C-to-C cables.
